🌟 Overview

PAL Optical needed a public website that matched the professionalism of their practice — something clean, fast, and easy to navigate for patients looking up hours, services, or contact details.

Built from scratch with no frameworks or page builders, the site is fully responsive, loads fast on any connection, and presents the practice in the best possible light across desktop and mobile.
