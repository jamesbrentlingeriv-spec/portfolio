Interactive Price List

A comprehensive, interactive price list for PAL Optical lenses, materials, and coatings — with real-time search, category filtering, and a built-in OptiCalc integration.

🌟 Overview

The PAL Optical Interactive Price List was built to replace a static printed price sheet that staff had to manually update and reprint every time pricing changed. The new version lives in the browser, updates instantly, and lets anyone on the floor find exactly what they need in seconds.

It covers the full catalog — progressive lenses, single vision, bifocals, materials (plastic, poly, high-index), and coatings — all in one searchable, filterable interface with a dark mode designed for long shifts.
